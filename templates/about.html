Hello and Welcome to my final project for CS50!


My name is Auriane and for the past few months I have been taking a computer science class online and this is my final project. I wanted this project to be meaningful as well as useful. So I decided, to link two of my passions which are social and environmental justice with coding!


In the past, I have used the Power Cube to analyze power dynamic created by participatory methods for a research project. As I was implementing this framework to my analysis, I created an excel spread sheet to save the different elements of my analysis on the same page, which would allow me to have a global vision of my work. The purpose of this webapp is to offer a structure that allows you to type your analysis directly into the form that represents the Power Cube framework and to render a styled document of your analysis.


If you have any comments or suggestions please feel free to connect with me at auriane.vez at gmail.com. I am at the beginning of my coding journey, so any suggestion to upgrade this app are welcomed!
